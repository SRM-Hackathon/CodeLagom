Blind Reader

BE THE BEACON OF HOPE IN THEIR LIVES.LET US SUPPORT THEM IN EVERY STEP WE TAKE

  • Make all books, texts, voices and "emotions" in those voices accessible to blind as well as blind-deaf in real time braille.
  • Navigate them round the clock

OUR TECHNOLOGY

  • Wearable gadget with real time braille transmission
  • Image to dynamic braille via text conversion
  • Audio to dynamic braille for deafblind assistance
Also, any pdf can be encoded into our device friendly text to be streamed to the blind person in the form of braille. The device mimics the raised and non-raised dots that the braille language uses. This makes all books accessible to blind and blind-deaf. Also we are trying to implement a tap/knock interface for people who are both blind and deaf to aid them with the interface.

Supporting Statistics:

-Of the 37 million people across the globe who are blind, over 15 million are from India. -AudioBooks are the primary way of access to books that haven't been translated in braille, although they are fluent in reading braille, and gives the actual reading experience to blind. -NARUC ( National Association for Regulatory Utility Commission ) estimated two decades ago that over 70,000 to 100,000 are both deaf and blind.
